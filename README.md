## Features

### Per-exercise weight tracking
Log weight and reps for **any exercise, independently**. See your bench press, squat, deadlift, and OHP charted separately with full history. No cramming everything into one confusing graph.

### Composite strength score
wtracker calculates a single **composite strength score** based on your performance across your tracked lifts, normalized and weighted. Watch your number climb. Know where you actually stand.

`Beginner` → `Novice` → `Intermediate` → `Advanced` → `Elite`

### iOS and Android
Built in Flutter. One codebase, two platforms, zero compromises. It runs smooth. It looks good. It doesn't ask for your email.
